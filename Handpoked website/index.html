Frequently Asked Questions

Does handpoke hurt more?

+

Handpoke tattoos are often described as gentler than machine tattoos, though pain varies depending on placement and individual tolerance.

How long does healing take?

+

Most tattoos heal on the surface within 2–3 weeks, while complete healing beneath the skin can take up to 6 weeks.

How do I book a session?

+

Fill out the consultation form above. I'll review your idea and get back to you within 1–2 days.

Do you accept custom designs?

+

Yes. Every piece is designed collaboratively to create something personal, timeless, and uniquely yours.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

+

Eat well, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol the day before. Wear comfortable clothing that allows access to the tattoo area.

What is your aftercare process?

+

Detailed aftercare instructions will be provided after your appointment to ensure proper healing and long-lasting results.
